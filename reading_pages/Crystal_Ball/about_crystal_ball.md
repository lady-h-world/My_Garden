Now let's enter the cave to see Lady H.'s crystal ball. Could you imagine a cave could have such tranquil scenery inside? I mean, witnessing flowers, a lake, and a gazebo bathed in moonlight, all within a cave?! Lady H. made this arrangement because the crystal ball is constantly too bright, and yes, the crystal ball is that "moon". 
  
Lady H. acquired this magical crystal ball from a witch who had used it for decades. Originally, the ball would glow and dim, but one day it became permanently bright and couldn't be turned off. The witch decided to get a replacement and brought the old crystal ball to a flea market, giving it away for free. Coincidentally, Lady H. was at the same market searching for a light to adorn her cave. After exploring the entire market without finding anything suitable, she finally came across the glowing crystal ball carried by the witch.

It turned out to be quite the bargain! Lady H. got it for free, and it's still remarkably powerful. Although she can't wield it quite like a witch, the crystal ball's default ability is to gather data from any corner of the universe. Over the years, Lady H. has also discovered a few more magical powers it possesses, which we are excited to reveal to you now!
